## Ad Creative Notes

### Video Ad Specs:
- Hook in first 3 seconds (problem or curiosity)
- Show the transformation (before/after)
- Keep it authentic (not polished/corporate)
- Include captions (80% watch without sound)
- End with clear CTA

### Image Ad Specs:
- Bold headline that stops the scroll
- Clean design (not cluttered)
- Include the key transformation
- Brand colors: Black, White, Green (#22c55e)

### Targeting Suggestions:
- Interest: Business coaching, Life coaching, Online coaching
- Interest: Digital marketing, Content marketing, Email marketing
- Interest: Entrepreneurship, Small business
- Behavior: Online course buyers, Digital product purchasers
- Lookalike: Website visitors, Email list, Purchasers

### Budget Recommendations:
- Test phase: $20-50/day per ad set
- Scale winners: 20% budget increase every 3-4 days
- Kill losers: <1% CTR or >$5 CPL after $50 spend
